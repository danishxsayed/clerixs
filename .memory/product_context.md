# Clerixs: Product Context

## Overview
Clerixs is a specialized **Clinic Management SaaS** designed for doctors, lab technicians, and clinic administrators. The platform streamlines medical workflows from patient registration to prescription management and billing.

## Core Mission
- **Digitization**: Replace paper-based records with structured, searchable medical data.
- **Workflow Efficiency**: Reduce the time spent on administrative tasks like bill generation and drug dosage instructions.
- **Data Integrity**: Ensure every patient interaction follows a strict organizational hierarchy (Multi-tenant) and storage policy.
- **Brand & Community**: Building a transparent mission through professional storytelling, educational blog content, and a focus on humanizing healthcare.

## Key Features
- **Patient Management**: Central profile tracking with registration codes (e.g., `PT-1001`).
- **Clinical Documents**: Secure storage for X-Rays, Lab Reports, and patient files.
- **Prescription System**: Template-driven medicine prescribing to minimize typing and errors.
- **Lab Module**: Internal lab order tracking and external report management.
- **Financials**: Integrated billing, invoice tracking, and payment recording.
- **Educational Hub**: Integrated blog for healthcare technology insights and practice management guides.

## Target Audience
- Small to Mid-sized private clinics and diagnostic centers.
- Multi-branch diagnostic labs.
- Modern healthcare professionals looking to trade paperwork for peak efficiency.
